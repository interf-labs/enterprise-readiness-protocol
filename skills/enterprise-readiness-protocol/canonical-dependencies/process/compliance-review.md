# Compliance Review

Enterprise compliance team assesses whether the vendor meets applicable regulatory requirements — HIPAA, GDPR, PCI-DSS, SOX, FedRAMP. Often runs in parallel with the security review but involves different stakeholders and criteria.

## Template

- **what:** Compliance review for applicable regulatory frameworks
- **ready:** Compliance review completed and approved for all applicable regulations

## Matches

Use this type when vendor says: "compliance review", "regulatory assessment", "HIPAA compliance", "GDPR review", "PCI-DSS certification", "SOX controls", "FedRAMP"
