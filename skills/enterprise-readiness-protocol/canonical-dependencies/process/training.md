# Training

Enterprise needs to train end-users on the new system or workflow introduced by the vendor. May include live sessions, self-paced modules, or train-the-trainer programs depending on the scale of rollout.

## Template

- **what:** Training program for end-users (~2 hour session)
- **ready:** All designated end-users have completed training and can independently use the system

## Matches

Use this type when vendor says: "user training", "end-user enablement", "training sessions", "train-the-trainer", "onboarding workshop", "learning program"
