# Executive Sponsor

Senior leader who champions the initiative and can unblock cross-department coordination. Critical for enterprise rollouts that span multiple teams or require budget approval beyond a single department's authority.

## Template

- **what:** Executive sponsor to champion the initiative across departments
- **ready:** Sponsor identified and has communicated priority to relevant teams
